Why is Vancouver called Hollywood North?

With close proximity to Los Angeles, longstanding, stable and competitive tax incentives and government support, and a community that nurtures creativity, Vancouver is the ideal locale for any Film or TV production.

More than 60,000 direct and indirect jobs are generated by film and TV production in B.C.; more than 80 percent of these are in Metro Vancouver. Vancouver has the largest cluster of top VFX and Animation studios in the world, including Sony Pictures Imageworks, Industrial Light & Magic (ILM), Animal Logic and others.

Film and TV production volume in B.C. reached $3.4 billion in the 2018-19 fiscal year, according to the Canadian Media Producers Association's (CMPA) Profile 2019 report released in April 2020. The vast majority of activity in B.C.'s film industry — $2.82 billion — was service work for foreign productions.
